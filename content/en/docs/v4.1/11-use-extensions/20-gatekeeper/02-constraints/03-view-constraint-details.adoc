This section describes how to view constraint details.


== Prerequisites


* {empty}
include::../../../../_custom-en/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-prer-requiredPermission_new.adoc[]

* **Gatekeeper** should have been installed and enabled.

== Steps

include::../../../../_custom-en/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-oper-openClusterPage.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../_ks_components-en/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. Click the name of a constraint in the constraint list to open its details page.

. In the **Attributes** area on the left side of the constraint details page, view the basic information of the constraint.
+
--

[%header,cols="2,5"]
|===
|Parameter |Description

|Cluster
|The cluster to which the constraint belongs.

|Creation Time
|The creation time of the constraint.

|===
--

. In the **Violations** tab on the right side of the constraint details page, view the violation information.
